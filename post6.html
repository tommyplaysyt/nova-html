Nova: Cherry Ring - Announcement!

Posted on: December 22, 2024

By: Nova99 Dev Team



Look at how BEAUTIFUL it is!

Nova: Cherry Ring is basic Minecraft Survival, reimagined!


What is it?

Cherry Ring is a highly unique server, taking ideas from the Box and Vanilla Survival genre. The server is set in a ring, surrounded by mountainous terrain covered in Cherry Trees! The map is confined to the area within the mountains, making for a unique survival-box twist.


Your Goal:

Team up, or start a solo adventure. Make do with the resources within the ring to become the most dominant player before the next server wipe!


Server Wipes

Every week (subject to change), the server map wipes completely back to the beginning! Content Updates come every wipe, with small map changes and general tweaks weekly! Virtual Mobs and Currency are NOT included in wipes.


Petal Currency

The server wouldn't be unique if our currency wasn't PETALS! Gain currency by opening crates, claiming your Free Chest, and best of all: trading Pink Petals!

10 Pink Petals = $1

1 Pink Petal = $0.1

On average every wipe, there is about 25,000 worth of natueal Pink Petals on the map.


How to join?

Join through Minehut by running /join CherryRings or join directly through the IP:

cherry.novamc.net
