Fertilia: Update 7

Posted on: December 6, 2025

By: BYTE Play Devs!

The Christmas Event: Part 1 is here!

Added

Festive Crop Variant

Farmed globally, the new FESTIVE crop variant is here!

Selling these yields Christmas Coins!

The value of these will always be $1, but can be increased by upgrading!




Candy Canes

Also drops globally!

Use candycanes in the new Christmas Upgrades!




Tree Decorations

Used to decorate your tree!

Different decorations award more points!

Christmas Globe - 3 points

Christmas Stocking - 12 points

Christmas Candle - 36 points

Every hour depending on your decoration points, receive TONS OF ITEMS!






Christmas Upgrades

Boost your way with these limited upgrades!

Includes drop chance upgrades, santa shop discounts, and the christmas coins multi!

You'll want tons of candycanes for these!




Mailbox

A new feature fit for the holiday season!

Send items to your friends or foes, even when they're offline!

You may hold up to 40 items in your mailbox before it gets full.





Santa's Presents

He's filling up YOUR MAILBOX!

Every 30 minutes, santa is sending presents to your mailbox!

Keep an eye out!




Tree Decorations

Use your decorations here!

Get rewarded hourly based on decoration points!






Leaderboard Rewards!

The first MEDALLION CHARM of this season!

Most decorated trees for the whole week will be given SPECIAL REWARDS!

#1 - MEDALLION charm, 6x Exclusive Chorus Flower

#2 - SYNTHESIS charm, 20x Enchant Relic, 3x Exclusive Chorus Flower, 4x INSANE Water Sprinkler

#3 - 15x Enchant Relic, 2x Exclusive Chorus Flower

Good Luck!




Apple Competition

Another MEDALLION CHARM for you all!

The player with the highest valued APPLE will receive rewards!

#1 - MEDALLION charm, 6x Exclusive Chorus Flower

#2 - SYNTHESIS charm, 20x Enchant Relic, 3x Exclusive Chorus Flower, 4x INSANE Water Sprinkler

#3 - 15x Enchant Relic, 2x Exclusive Chorus Flower

Good Luck!




Fixed

- Mini Bags can now be bulk opened with Sneak + Right Click

- Exotic Fruit Tier 7 trade now gives 2x Bastion Fruit seed instead of 1

- Halved price of Crop Duster in Exotic Fruits
