Collect All Blocks - Announcement!

Posted on: November 9, 2024

By: Nova Devs

Welcome to our first ever Nova Networks Blog Post!

We're excited to announce our newest gamemode, Collect All Blocks!


What is it?

Collect All Blocks! is a unique box server experience with tons of progression.


What's the goal?

Collect all 132+ unique blocks, max out your upgrades, unlock expensive armor to unlock a killstreak rampage!


Is it P2W?

Not at all! CAB! doesn't even have a Server Store yet! Enjoy a very F2P, grindy gamemode free of Pay 2 Win players ruining your experience!


How to join?

Join through minehut by running /join BlockCollect or join directly through the IP:

cab.novamc.net
