Re-branding Announcement

Posted on: June 6, 2025

By: BYTE Play



Why the Name Change?

Nova Networks served us well as a short-term placeholder; a name used for general branding in our beginnings. It never reflected where were going or what were trying to build. As we expand beyond a single server into a studio of ideas, it’s time for something more distinct, scalable, and creative.

That’s why we’re now called BYTE Play—a name built for the future, just like the games we create.


What does BYTE Play mean?

At BYTE Play, we’re crafting worlds one byte at a time. It’s a tenure to both digital design and the playful spirit behind everything we build. Whether it’s bite-sized minigames or expansive survival realms, we believe in quality, creativity, and connection.

As we grow, so will the brand—BYTE Play is only the beginning. One day, we might just be known as BYTE.


What changes for you?

Nothing, really—except everything gets cooler. You’ll still see all your favorite servers but now with more polish, better ideas, and a bigger vision behind them. Expect more updates, more innovation, and more fun.


When will this happen?

It'll be a slow, but gradual process that starts today. So far, we've rebranded this website and the webstore. Our recently launched game Fertilia also displays this new branding.
