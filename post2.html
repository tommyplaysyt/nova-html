Collect All Blocks: Update 1!

Posted on: November 14, 2024

By: Nova99 Dev Team

Welcome to our first ever update to Collect All Blocks, and our first ever developer blog post!

This update primarily focuses on hotfixes and bug squashing, but our team have greatly assisted in pushing out new content for you all!


Added

New Common Blocks

5 new Common Blocks have been added to the Mine!

The Dried Coral set has a high spawn chance.




New Rare Blocks

4 new Rare Blocks have been added to the Mine!

They give a lot of money!

These are the rarest blocks ever added to the game, with a chance of just 0.01%!

Can you collect them all?




Team War

We've added a timer to the current Team War!

This current Team War will end November 29.

Keep an eye out for the next Team War, it's Christmas-themed!




AFK Rewards

Every 10 minutes in the AFK Area, you'll now receive 1x Crystal Key!

A great way to support the server, while also receiving rewards!




Fixed

- Global Active Event is fixed

- Rare Blocks added to sidebar

- Increased gem cap to 10m

- Global Gem Booster cost per tier increases more per upgrade, with a slight buff

- Mastery Potion dupe fix

- Team Deposit Gems dupe fix

- Removed mastery potion from Tier 2 Travelling Merchant table

- Fixed many issues with Teams
