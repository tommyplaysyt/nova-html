Collect All Blocks: Update 3!

Posted on: December 1, 2024

By: Nova Dev Team

Happy Holidays! We're cramming in TONS of content during the holiday season!

Ads in the Minehut Lobby are greatly appreciated, but not at all required!


Added

Recent Average Price

All items now have a "Recent Average Price"!

This price is gathered from the average sell prices on the Marketplace.

/marketplace

/rap - View the RAP Value of your inventory!

/itemrap - View the RAP Value of the item in hand!

Expect item values to fluctuate with inflation at the start.

Over time, items will stabilise and values will be harder to manipulate by bad actors.

You may be auto-marketplace banned if we notice your involvement with abuse of item values.





RAP Leaderboard

A new leaderboard has arrived..

Climb the leaderboard by collecting items and increasing the value of your inventory.

Updates every 5 minutes!




New Christmas Items

4 limited items have been added:

Including the Christmas Paper, Christmas Box, Christmas Bow used to craft Presents, obtained from random drops!

Including the Christmas Cookie, offering Speed II for 1 minute, obtained from Presents!
